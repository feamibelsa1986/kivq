## What is a Toulmin Thesis?

The Toulmin Thesis is a form of argumentative thesis that uses logical reasoning and evidence to support a claim. It typically includes the following components:

- **Claim:** The statement or position the writer is arguing for.
- **Grounds:** The evidence or facts that support the claim.
- **Warrant:** The explanation of why the evidence supports the claim.
- **Backing:** Additional support to strengthen the argument.
- **Qualifier:** The degree of certainty with which the claim is made.
- **Rebuttal:** Acknowledging counterarguments and explaining why they are not valid.
